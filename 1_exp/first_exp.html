About Us

GCT is an autonomous institution affliated to anna university. One of the oldest and reputed institute in Coimbatore. This great college was started in the year of 1942, which is celebrated the platinum jubliee this year. It offers both UG and PG courses, Phd courses also. The famous companies which offers placement are Zoho, TCS, Deeshaw, WIPRO etc. The college has a greeny atmosphere which gives fresh environment for students. GCT is a Band Ainstitution. And also contained a NBA acredation.GCT is in 6th place in NIRF ranking. Our college library contains 10,000 books for student reference. Not only course textbooks it also magazines, journals, newspapers story books, novels etc.There also available libraries for each department.

Clubs

There are numerous clubs in our college for developing ourselves in each fields.


Tamil Mandram:

Tamil Mandram is a very big club with more than 150 students from 1st year to 4th year excisting foo more than 40 years.This club includes fine arts club, students club providing lots of sessions to enhance our dancing, acting, speech, drawing skills. There are two wonderful funcitons every year: Muthamzhil Vizha and Sangamam to show case our talents.


Rotaract:

Rotaract is a professional club with more than 200 students from 1st year to 4th year. This club is also an international clubs completed more number of projects based on club service, community service, international service.This club helps us to enhance our professionalism.It also helps us to do services for people all over the world. There is an installation ceremony called 'Inherit' for each batch of students to get posted in different position in the club.


Y's Men Service Club:

Y's Men Service Club is an international club with more than 200 students for providing services for students and to develop themselves. Students will learn how to organize events and how to guide the juniors to grow their skills.The former president Mr. Anandakumar from Mechanical department had raised this club to the greater height. He is an one man army dedicated himself for whole to this club.This club has one fortunate of interviewing Mr. Mayilsaami Annadurai for the Engineer's day.


SAE and LDS

Society for Automotive Engineers is an International organisation with its motto "The engineering society for advancing mobility Land Sea Air and Space"is serving as a platform for students to explore globally and to cope up with the advancements in the techno-world.It is a professional engineering society to imbibe the sundamentals and applications of engineering field.
Literary and Debating Society - the best club for developing our communication skills. The yearly event conducted by this club is 'Confluence' consists of lots of events like JAM, SHIPWRECK, POEM writing, etc.


Not only the above clubs there are lots clubs like Green Club, AAE also. Other than clubs the SPORTS achievements are also great.Our college secured lots of national level and state level prices and rewards for all sports.There is no trainer for them, but the seniors are their teachers. The best senior junior relationship is so helpful for us to study and achieve more.
